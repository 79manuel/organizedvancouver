Wardrobe Consultation

Some people are uncomfortable with the idea of allowing another person access to their closet. But we can make it fun, helpful and purposeful. We want you to wear and feel good in most of the clothes hanging in your closet. We provide an in-home service to review your current wardrobe with you, and give you an objective opinion about each item. We help you put together new outfits using your existing wardrobe. Based on your lifestyle, we also make a list of items you could consider adding to complete a perfect wardrobe for you.
